Continuum is a confidential closing room for private-equity secondaries, built on the Canton Network

We help secondary advisors and fund administrators run continuation-fund closes and settle them in a single atomic transaction.

02  The transaction

What a continuation deal is

01A fund is ending; one company is still worth holding.
02The GP moves it into a new continuation vehicle.
03Each LP elects: take cash now, or roll into the new fund.
04A secondary buyer provides fresh capital.

03  The structure

The GP is on both sides

sellsOld fund
GP
managesNew vehicle

The process relies on independent checks so the price and terms don't depend on the conflicted party.

An independent valuer prices the asset
A sealed auction discovers the price
The LPAC reviews fairness and consents
Each LP elects privately

04  The problem

Today, integrity depends on the conflicted party

LeakageSealed bids and LP elections sit in a data room the GP controls.
Partial closeThe close is separate wires and signatures; one failed leg leaves the deal half-closed.
Unverifiable recordThe audit trail is reconstructed afterward from emails and PDFs.

Every check assumes the GP's coordination is neutral.

05  The solution

The same checks, enforced by the ledger

Off-ledger today
On Canton
GP-controlled data roomPer-party privacy — each participant is projected only its own slice, by the ledger, not the app.
Separate wires and signaturesOne atomic transaction — units, cash, rolled units, burn: all legs or none.
GP coordinates every stepEach party signs with its own key — no shared database under the conflicted party.
Record reconstructed afterwardFour on-ledger proofs — valuation, fairness, consent, auction certificate; anyone entitled can verify.

Native to Canton: sub-transaction privacy · atomic settlement · selective disclosure · no central operator.

A2  Appendix

Module
What it does
Continuum.DealRoot state machine: opens the room, runs Consented → Electing → Closed, hosts the atomic Close.
Continuum.ValuationIndependent valuer anchors NAV with a real signature; fairness opinion. Self-dealing barred on-ledger.
Continuum.ConsentLPAC fairness review — the signed conflict-waiver the Close consumes.
Continuum.AuctionSealed bids, each a peer- and GP-blind private contract; a marker leaks only that a bid exists.
Continuum.ElectionPer-LP cash / roll choice, private to that LP; a contentless marker feeds aggregates.
Continuum.ClearingLead filled to capacity, syndicate pro-rata; asserts the deal is not undersubscribed.
Continuum.IssuanceAntecedent-DAG mint gate: no units until valuation, fairness, consent and auction cert all exist.
Continuum.SettlementPre-authorised transfer legs, executed inside the one atomic Close.
Continuum.RegistryInstrument registry + co-signed delegations; mints and reserves CV-unit and cash holdings.
Continuum.CredentialReusable, revocable Qualified-Purchaser eligibility a returning buyer reuses across deals.
Continuum.DocumentOn-ledger sha256 anchoring; every document hash flows through hashOf so it re-verifies.
