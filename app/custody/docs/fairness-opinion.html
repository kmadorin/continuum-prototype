Fairness Opinion

Project Continuum CV I, L.P. — GP-Led Continuation Vehicle

At the request of the LP Advisory Committee (the "LPAC") of Meridian Growth Fund III, we have reviewed the proposed transfer of limited-partnership interests into Project Continuum CV I, L.P. and the pricing thereof. This letter sets out our opinion as to whether the proposed clearing price is fair, from a financial point of view, to the limited partners electing to sell.

Basis of Review

We have considered the Independent Valuation Report prepared by Kroll Valuation Services dated 30 June 2026, which concludes a fair value of $500.0 million within a range of $480.0M – $520.0M; the tabulated results of the competitive sealed-bid auction; and the terms of the proposed purchase agreement. The clearing price of 96% of reference NAV ($480.0 million) falls within the independent valuer's stated range.

Opinion

Subject to the assumptions and limitations described herein, it is our opinion that, as of the date hereof, the consideration to be received by the electing limited partners pursuant to the proposed transaction is fair, from a financial point of view, to such limited partners.
