Purchase and Sale Agreement

relating to interests in Meridian Growth Fund III transferred to Project Continuum CV I, L.P.

This Purchase and Sale Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of 30 June 2026 between Meridian Growth Fund III, as transferor (the "Old Fund"), and Project Continuum CV I, L.P., as transferee (the "Continuation Vehicle"), acting through Fireblocks GP as sponsor.

Economic Terms

Reference NAV (as-of 30 Jun 2026)$500,000,000
Clearing price (% of reference NAV)96.0%
Aggregate purchase price$480,000,000
CV units issued (at $1.00)480,000,000 units
Independent valuation range$480.0M – $520.0M (Kroll)
Closing date30 June 2026

Key Provisions

  1. The purchase price shall equal the reference NAV multiplied by the clearing price established through the competitive sealed-bid auction, being 96.0% of $500.0 million.
  2. Completion is conditional upon (i) the Independent Valuation Report of Kroll Valuation Services, (ii) the Fairness Opinion of the LPAC, and (iii) LPAC consent having been recorded on the ledger.
  3. Consideration shall be settled atomically against issuance of CV units to electing rolling partners and cash to electing selling partners, in a single ledger transaction.
  4. Each transferring interest is assigned free and clear of encumbrances upon settlement.
